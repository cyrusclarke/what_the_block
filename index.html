The Blockchain

Blockchain is the next evolution of the internet.

As we shift from an internet of information to an internet of value, distributed ledger technology is poised to re-shape culture, the way economies function, and the ways that politics and governments work.

It will remove artifical borders and friction, revolutionise health and life-long learning, accelerate the re-invention of work and allow a host of autonomous smart objects to go about their work – while putting us back in control of our own data and identities.

Design Thinking
